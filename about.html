ABOUT US

Our Story

Bringing exceptional flavors from the finest kitchens to your doorstep since 2020.

From Passion to Plate

RoastBite was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves access to extraordinary food without leaving their home. What started as a small kitchen operation in downtown New York has grown into a beloved platform connecting food lovers with the city's most talented chefs.

Every dish on our platform is carefully curated. We partner exclusively with restaurants that share our commitment to quality ingredients, thoughtful preparation, and exceptional taste.

Our delivery team is trained to handle each order with the same care as a fine-dining server — because we believe the last mile is just as important as the first ingredient.
